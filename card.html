Killer Whale

Killer Whales use vocalization to communicate with each other, but each pod has it’s own unique ‘accent,’ which makes it easy for Orcas to identify members of their own pods.

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus orca
  • Mass : Male: 8,000 lbs (Adult), Female: 3,000 lbs (Adult)
  • Length: Male: 20 – 26 ft., Female: 16 – 23 ft.
  • Habitat: Found in all oceans, most abundant in cooler waters.

Killer whales are highly social animals. They are the most widely distributed mammals, other than humans, and adapt very well to any climate. Apex predators, Orcas will prey on almost any animal they find in the sea, in the air over the water or along the coastline.
